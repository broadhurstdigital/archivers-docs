# Inviting Team Members

Admins can invite anyone to join their Organisation in The Archiver. Here's how.

:::note
Inviting members requires the Team or Enterprise plan, and you must be an Admin.
:::

---

## How to invite someone

1. Go to **Settings** (click your avatar → Settings)
2. Click the **Organisation** tab
3. Click **Invite member**
4. Enter the person's **email address**
5. Select their **role** — Admin, General, or Viewer (see [Roles & Permissions](/teams/roles-and-permissions))
6. Click **Send invitation**

An email is sent to the invited person with a link to accept the invitation.

---

## What happens next

The invited person will receive an email from The Archiver with a link to accept the invitation.

- If they **already have an account**, they click the link and are added to the organisation immediately
- If they **don't have an account**, they're prompted to create one first, then they're added

Once accepted, they can log in and access the shared archive.

---

## Tracking pending invitations

Invitations that haven't been accepted yet appear in the **Invitations** section of the Organisation settings. You can see who's been invited, when, and whether they've accepted.

---

## Resending an invitation

If the invite email didn't arrive (check spam folders first!):

1. Go to **Settings → Organisation → Invitations**
2. Find the pending invitation
3. Click **Resend**

---

## Cancelling an invitation

To cancel a pending invitation before it's accepted:

1. Go to **Settings → Organisation → Invitations**
2. Find the invitation
3. Click **Cancel invitation**

The invitation link will no longer work.

---

## Removing a member

To remove someone from your Organisation:

1. Go to **Settings → Organisation → Members**
2. Find the member
3. Click the **⋮** menu next to their name
4. Select **Remove member**
5. Confirm

:::warning
Removing a member immediately revokes their access to the archive. They will not be notified automatically — let them know beforehand if appropriate.
:::

The removed member's contributions (uploaded files, Accessions, Dossiers) remain in the archive. Their content is not deleted.

---

## Member limits

| Plan | Maximum members |
|------|----------------|
| Free | Solo only (no organisation) |
| Pro | Solo only (no organisation) |
| Team | Up to 10 members |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

If you've reached your member limit, you'll need to upgrade to add more people.
