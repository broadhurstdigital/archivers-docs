# Quick Analysis vs. Full Analysis

When you're ready to analyse a Dossier, The Archiver offers two modes: **Quick Analysis** and **Full Analysis**. Here's what each does and when to use them.

---

## Quick Analysis

**What it does:**
The AI reads all the files in your Dossier in a single pass and produces a concise summary with highlights.

**Output includes:**
- A narrative overview
- Key themes and subjects
- Important people and places
- Significant quotes

**How long it takes:** Usually 30–60 seconds.

**Best for:**
- Getting a fast overview of a new collection
- Checking whether a set of files is relevant to your research
- Smaller Dossiers (up to ~50 files)
- When you need something quick, not comprehensive

---

## Full Analysis

**What it does:**
The AI runs a multi-pass analysis:
1. **Survey pass** — reads all files and builds an overview
2. **You review** — you can approve or adjust the AI's reading before it continues
3. **Deep read** — the AI reads the most important files in much greater detail
4. **Packaging** — the AI assembles the final structured report

**Output includes all Quick Analysis outputs, plus:**
- Detailed narrative sections with citations
- A full chronological timeline
- Entity cards (people, organisations, places)
- Connections between items
- Key findings and conclusions
- Research gaps and unanswered questions
- Suggested follow-up questions

**How long it takes:** 3–10 minutes depending on the size of the Dossier.

**Best for:**
- In-depth research projects
- Large Dossiers (50+ files)
- When you need a publishable-quality summary
- Producing output for a Finding Aid or report

---

## Choosing between them

| | Quick | Full |
|--|-------|------|
| Speed | ~1 minute | 3–10 minutes |
| Depth | Overview | Comprehensive |
| Best for | Exploration | Research reports |
| Follow-up questions | Limited | Full support |

:::tip
**Start with Quick Analysis** to check you have the right files in your Dossier. If the overview looks good, run a Full Analysis for the detailed report.
:::

---

## Running the analysis

1. Open your Dossier
2. Click **Analyse**
3. Select **Quick Analysis** or **Full Analysis**
4. (Full Analysis only) Optionally enable **Intelligence Boost** — this uses a more powerful AI model for better results on complex collections, but takes longer
5. Click **Start**

The analysis runs in the background. You can leave the page and come back — the results will be waiting for you.
