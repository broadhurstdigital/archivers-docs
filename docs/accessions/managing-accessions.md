# Managing Accessions

The Accessions list is where you'll see all your uploaded batches of files. Here's how to navigate and manage them.

---

## The Accessions list

Click **Accessions** in the left sidebar to open the list. You'll see:

- A card or row for each accession
- Its **title**, **date range**, and **file count**
- Its **processing status** (Processing / Complete / Draft)
- The **date it was created**

---

## Sorting and filtering

Use the toolbar above the list to:

- **Sort** — by date created, name, or file count
- **Filter by status** — show only Complete, Draft, or Processing accessions
- **Search** — type a name to filter the list

---

## Opening an accession

Click on any accession to open it. Inside you'll find:

- A summary panel (title, description, dates, stats)
- The full list of files in the accession
- Each file's classification, transcript, and metadata

---

## Editing accession details

To edit the title, description, or date range of an accession:

1. Open the accession
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) next to the title
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

---

## Adding more files

You can add more files to an existing accession:

1. Open the accession
2. Click **Add files**
3. Upload files as normal — they'll be processed and added to the accession

---

## Deleting an accession

:::warning
Deleting an accession permanently removes all its files and metadata. **This cannot be undone.**
:::

1. Open the accession
2. Click the **⋮** (three dots) menu in the top-right
3. Select **Delete accession**
4. Confirm the deletion

---

## Draft accessions

If you started creating an accession but didn't finish, it appears as a **Draft** on your dashboard and in the Accessions list. Click it to continue where you left off.

Draft accessions that are more than 30 days old may be automatically cleaned up.
