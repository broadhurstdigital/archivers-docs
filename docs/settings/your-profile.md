# Your Profile

Your profile settings control your name, email address, and password for The Archiver.

---

## Accessing your profile

1. Click your **avatar** (initials or photo) in the top-right corner of the app
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown
3. You'll land on the **Profile** tab

---

## Updating your name

1. Click in the **Name** field
2. Type your new name
3. Click **Save changes**

Your name appears in the app's top bar and (if you're on a Team plan) is visible to other members of your organisation.

---

## Changing your email address

1. Click in the **Email** field
2. Enter your new email address
3. Click **Save changes**
4. A verification email is sent to your new address
5. Click the link in that email to confirm the change

:::warning
You must verify the new email address before the change takes effect. Until you click the verification link, you'll still log in with your old email.
:::

---

## Changing your password

1. Click **Change password**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Confirm the new password
5. Click **Update password**

:::tip
Use a unique password for The Archiver. A password manager (like 1Password or Bitwarden) makes this easy.
:::

---

## Deleting your account

:::warning
Deleting your account permanently removes all your files, accessions, and data. **This cannot be undone.**
:::

If you need to delete your account, contact us at [support@archivers.ai](mailto:support@archivers.ai).
