# Usage & Plan

The **Usage & Plan** screen shows how much of your monthly allowance you've used and gives you information about your current subscription.

---

## Accessing usage information

1. Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click the **Usage & Plan** tab

Or, click the **usage bar** at the top of the Dashboard home screen.

---

## Understanding your usage

### Items processed
Each file you upload and process counts as one **item**. Your plan includes a monthly item allowance:

| Plan | Items per month |
|------|----------------|
| Free | 20 |
| Pro | 250 |
| Team | 1,000 |
| Enterprise | 5,000 |

The counter resets on the 1st of each month.

### Storage
The total space used by all your files across all accessions.

| Plan | Storage |
|------|---------|
| Free | 500 MB |
| Pro | 10 GB |
| Team | 100 GB |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

### AI usage
Explore sessions and Dossier analyses use additional AI processing. The usage screen shows how much AI processing you've used this month.

---

## Data retention

:::warning
On the **Free plan**, files are automatically deleted after **7 days**. Upgrade to a paid plan to keep your archive permanently.
:::

| Plan | Data kept for |
|------|--------------|
| Free | 7 days |
| Pro | Forever |
| Team | Forever |
| Enterprise | Forever |

---

## Upgrading your plan

To upgrade:
1. Click **Upgrade plan** on the Usage & Plan screen
2. Choose your new plan
3. Enter payment details
4. Your new allowances take effect immediately

---

## Cancelling your subscription

To cancel your subscription, click **Manage subscription** on the Usage & Plan screen. You'll be taken to the billing portal where you can cancel. Your data remains accessible until the end of your current billing period.
