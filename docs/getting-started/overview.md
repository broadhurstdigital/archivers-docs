# Dashboard Tour

When you first log in to The Archiver, you'll see the **Dashboard** — the central hub of your archive. Here's a quick tour of what you'll find.

---

## The sidebar

The left-hand sidebar contains the main navigation:

- **Dashboard** — your home screen with recent activity and stats
- **Accessions** — all your uploaded batches of files
- **Fonds** — your archival hierarchy (collections, series, sub-series)
- **Search** — full-text search across your entire archive
- **Dossiers** — thematic research folders
- **Explore** — AI research chat
- **Settings** — your profile, AI instructions, and plan details

:::note
Fonds and Dossiers are available on paid plans only. If you're on the free tier, you'll see an upgrade prompt when you click these sections.
:::

---

## The Dashboard home screen

The main area shows:

### Stats strip
At the top, you'll see your current usage — how many items you've processed this month, how much storage you're using, and how many items remain in your allowance.

### Recent accessions
A list of your most recently uploaded batches (called **Accessions**). Click any accession to open it and see its files.

### Draft accessions
Accessions you've started but not yet completed appear here. You can pick up where you left off.

---

## Top bar

The top bar contains:
- **Search** — click the magnifying glass to search your entire archive instantly
- **Your account menu** — click your avatar to access settings, view your plan, or log out
